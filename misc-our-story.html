Founders

Lucky Shrub was started by a husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love for plants. Jason is the garden architect. He created and oversees all designs while managing his team of landscapers. Maria manages all the marketing for the company and oversees the nursery.

Mission & Values

We believe that great gardens are built on harmony—between people, plants, and the environment. Our mission is to design sustainable, functional, and beautiful outdoor spaces that bring everyday joy. At the heart of our work are three core values: sustainability, using native plants and eco-conscious vision and lifestyle; and collaboration, working closely with every client from the initial concept through to completion to ensure every garden reflects a shared passion for nature.

Growth and Achievements

From our small workshop in Tucson to a thriving design studio with a dedicated team of landscapers, horticulturists, and designers — our roots have grown deep. Over the years, we’ve helped transform residential courtyards, city rooftops, and community parks into lush, inviting environments. We’re proud to be known for our modern yet natural aesthetic — blending structure with softness, and artistry with practicality.
